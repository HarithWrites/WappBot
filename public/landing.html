How It Works

Three steps. Fully automated.

From WhatsApp message to confirmed appointment — hands-free, every time.

01

Customer says "Hi"

Your customer sends a WhatsApp message. WappBot instantly greets them and guides them through booking — no waiting, no staff needed.

02

Bot collects details

Service, provider, date, and time — all collected through a natural WhatsApp conversation using smart interactive menus.

03

You approve & confirm

Review the booking in your dashboard. One click to approve — and the customer instantly receives a WhatsApp confirmation.
